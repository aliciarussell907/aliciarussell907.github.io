Pacific Rhododendron

Common names of the Pacific Rhododendron

The Pacific Rhododendron's scientific name is Rhododendron macrophyllum, but it is more commonly referred to as:

  • Coast Rhododendron
  • California Rhododendron
  • California Rosebay
  • Rhodies

Size Range

The Pacific Rhododendron can grow between 3 to 15 feet in height - possibly upwards of 25 feet if conditions allow. The height of each individual plant depends on how much sunlight they receive. The more shaded the plant is, the taller it will grow.

Where It Can Be Found

The Pacific Rhododendron is found west of the Cascades from southern British Columbia to Northern California.

Preferred Climate

The Pacific Rhododendron prefers fairly dry open forests or well-draining, acidic soil with low nitrogen.
