History

NestCraft 3D

Let's take it to the beginning...

In 2019, Riley (our founder) bought her first 3D printer just for fun. When COVID sent everyone into social distancing, Riley began making a range of gifts and gadgets for her family and friends to stay connected in times of social distancing and add a little brightness to their days. Soon her friends began urging her to start a business selling her 3D printed home decor.

Riley took the first leap in late 2021 by selling her 3D printed pieces at craft fairs--each piece still made with immense love and care. By 2022, a local business asked if they could also sell her pieces in their storefront. Riley happily agreed, but it wasn't long until she realized this operation was becoming overwhelming for a team of one.

The team grew...

In June of 2023, Riley happily welcomed Lena to the team. Lena took care of all the administrative and social media aspects of NestCraft 3D while Riley continued working behind the printer. During a local craft fair in 2024, Riley met Jordan who wowed her with his eye for design. It wasn't long before he was invited to join the NestCraft 3D team. With a team of three, it was easier to manange day-to-day operations. So then we decided to take the next step and expand.

We launched our website...

In 2025, we decided to take our business nationwide and offer our products online. With this big step, we have been completely overhauling our process and designs offered. We still sell pre-printed designs in our local boutique and at crafts fairs, but our online orders are all printed to order allowing for a wider range of customizations. Our selection may be limited now, but we have big plans in store for next year! Thank you for all your continued support through our journey.
