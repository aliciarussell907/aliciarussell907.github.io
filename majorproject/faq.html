Frequently Asked Questions

Ordering a product from NestCraft 3D is as simple as filling out our order request form. We have our customers request their order because each piece is specially printed for you! We do not keep an inventory in stock to allow our customers to customized their pieces for an affordable price.

No need to fear! You will receive an invoice from us within two business days after submitting your request. If you have not received your invoice in that timeframe, please check your email's "junk" or "spam" folder. If you still cannot find it, please do not hesitate to contact us directly.

Because our product is not printed until after an order has been requested, we have to make sure we have enough material for any customizations. We do our best to keep plenty of filament on hand, but sometimes we get an unexpected influx of custom orders. In the event this happens, we will reach out to you the option to wait for our filament to be restocked or offer a different filament which may affect the total price.

PLA refers to the plant-based bioplastic filament we use to print our products with. It is made from renewable resources (like corn starch) which reduces our reliance on fossil fuels and petroleum-based plastics. We use this in all our products to help better protect the Earth on which all our nests reside.

We're still a small business and are in the process of getting rid of old, out-dated designs in favor for new ones that will provide even more functionality and joy for your nest. Jordan Maseko (our design specialist) has recently joined the team and has curated many new and exciting designs for us to bring to you! We are in the final stages of thoroughly testing each design to ensure it is stands up to the high standards you have come to know and expect from NestCraft 3D. You can catch a sneak peek of these designs at the bottom of our products page in the "Coming Soon" section. These designs are expected to be launch in early 2026, so be sure to check back soon!
