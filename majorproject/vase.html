Rose in a Vase

Add a touch of modern elegance and color to any space with this charming decorative set.

Starting at $50

Elevate your décor with this striking modern display piece featuring a bold geometric black vase paired with a vibrant 3D-printed red rose. The vase’s angular, faceted design adds a contemporary touch, while the bright red bloom and green stem introduce a playful pop of color that stands out beautifully against the minimalist form.

Crafted from durable, lightweight PLA, both the vase and rose are designed to maintain their shape and color over time—no watering, wilting, or maintenance required. Perfect as a desk accent, a gift for someone special, or a stylish centerpiece for any room.

Features:

  • Modern geometric vase design with clean, sculptural lines
  • Lightweight and long-lasting—never wilts or fades
  • Made from eco-friendly, durable PLA material
  • Ideal for home décor, office spaces, or gift-giving

Customizations available:

  • Silk red PLA for rose (+$10)
  • Marble PLA for vase (+$20)
  • Silk rainbow PLA for rose (+$12)
