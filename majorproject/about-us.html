NestCraft 3D

Meet The Team

Riley Hartman

Riley Hartman, founder of NestCraft 3D, launched this boutique 3D-printing studio dedicated to creating modern, customizable home décor in 2022. Inspired by the idea that every home should feel uniquely personal, Riley was determined to merge her passion for digital design with warm, handcrafted aesthetics.

At NestCraft 3D, Riley spends most of her time working behind the 3D printer ensuring every piece comes out perfect. During COVID, Riley spent a lot of time on her 3D printer making a range of gifts and gadgets for family and friends to stay connected in times of social distancing. She specializes in transforming digital concepts into beautifully sculpted décor pieces—ranging from unique and festive planters to artistic wall accents to functional décor.

When she’s not optimizing print settings or testing new filament blends, Riley draws inspiration from interior design trends, local art markets, and vintage furniture finds. Her mission through NestCraft 3D is simple: help people build spaces that feel creative, comfortable, and authentically their own.

Jordan Maseko

Jordan Maseko is the Design Specialist at NestCraft 3D, where he brings a sharp eye for modern functional design to the company’s growing catalog of 3D-printed home décor. Known for his calm, thoughtful presence and creative approach, Jordan focuses on blending contemporary style with practical everyday use.
At NestCraft 3D, Jordan develops digital models for custom décor pieces—including sculptural planters, geometric wall art, and unique figurines. His design philosophy revolves around versatility and simplicity: pieces that elevate a room without overwhelming it. He’s especially passionate about experimenting with layered patterns, natural-inspired shapes, and new filament materials that push the boundaries of what 3D-printed décor can feel like.

Outside of work, Jordan draws creative inspiration from streetwear, architecture, and urban color palettes. Whether sketching concepts in a café or refining details late into the night, he loves designing items that help people transform houses into expressive, personal spaces.

Lena Zhou

Lena Zhou is the Administrative Specialist at NestCraft 3D, where she keeps the company’s operations organized, efficient, and running smoothly behind the scenes. With a strong background in office management and workflow coordination, Lena is the backbone of daily communication and logistics throughout the studio.

At NestCraft 3D, Lena manages scheduling, vendor correspondence, order processing, inventory tracking, and client support. Her detail-oriented approach ensures every project—from custom décor commissions to large production runs—moves through the pipeline with clarity and precision. Colleagues know her for her calm professionalism, sharp organizational instincts, and ability to anticipate needs before anyone else has to ask.
